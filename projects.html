Hybrid Algorithms for Early-Stage Diabetes Prediction

This project uses a combination of **Particle Swarm Optimization (PSO)**, **Deep Neural Networks (DNN)**, and **Extreme Gradient Boosting (XGBoost)** to predict early-stage diabetes based on various health parameters. It focuses on improving prediction accuracy and implementing hybrid algorithms to enhance the detection process.

Technologies used: Python, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, PSO, DNN, XGBoost
